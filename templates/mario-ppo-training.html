Training: Value Loss

The following graph illustrates the change in Value Loss during a training iteration with about 10 million time steps. Value loss describes how well the policy model is able to predict the value at each state. This value fluctuates while learning and then stablizes as its prediction improves.

The orange and green lines show 2 different sessions of training using the same algorithm. The difference in the 2 training sessions illustrates how variable the training progression can be. Notice how both green and orange training sessions converge as they reach equilibrium on their Value Loss.

Training: Episode Reward

The following graph illustrates the change in Episode Reward during a training iteration with about 10 million steps. Episode Reward describes the mean reward from each episode in the training. The value fluctuates while learning but slowly increases as the policy improves.

The orange and green lines show 2 different sessions of training using the same algorithm. The differences again show how variable the training progression can be. Both training sessions converge in the Episode Rewards as they both improve their policies.
