Introduction to Reinforcement Learning

The G6 project team uses OpenAI Gym Retro Reinforcement Learning framework to train an agent to play through the classic Nintendo game, Super Mario Bros.

Reinforcement Learning (RL) is the process of training an agent to incrementally improve its action policy to get more rewards in an environment over the course of multiple trials. RL agents learn by assessing the consequence of their actions in the given environment. Games have easily defined reward values, so RL is commonly used in game environments.
