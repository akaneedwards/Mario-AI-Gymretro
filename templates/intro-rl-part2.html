Reward scenario

In order to train an RL agent, the rewards and penalties of the agent's environment must be defined. Each interaction has an assigned reward value. The rewards can be for numerical values such as increasing points, calculated by pulling out values from the game itself, or rewards for progressing through the environment, calculated by utilizing neural networks for image recognition on the visual environment. Negative rewards, or punishments, can also be utilized.


Image source: https://www.kdnuggets.com/2018/03/5-things-reinforcement-learning.html

The reward assignment structure can be referred to as a scenario. The scenario for Super Mario Bros consists of these basic parts:

  • move to the right and finish the level
  • minimize the number of lives lost
  • increase the number of points by collecting coins, killing enemies, etc. (optional)
