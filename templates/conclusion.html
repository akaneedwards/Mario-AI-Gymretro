Summary

Reinforcement Learning is a complex subject with a growing set of tools and practitioners. The tools output data created during RL training that help us assess the succcess of the algorithm and scenario configuration. The reward graph of the trained agent proves that the trained agent is gathering reward from its policy as the agent interacts with its environment. The graphs provide visual verification of the progress and success of the complex RL algorithms.
